How Advanced Options Work

These options modify how SLA calculations are performed to match different operational scenarios.

üìÖ Include Weekends

What it does: Controls whether weekends are included in the calculation baseline.

  • Enabled (default): Calculates against full calendar time (24/7)
  • Disabled: Calculates against weekdays only (5/7 of calendar time)

Use case: Disable for services that only operate on weekdays.

‚è∞ Analyze Work Time Impact

What it does: Shows the worst-case impact of downtime specifically on work hours.

  • SLA calculation: Still based on calendar time (industry standard)
  • Impact analysis: Shows what happens if all downtime occurs during work hours
  • Availability: Displays calendar SLA percentage with "(calendar)" label

Use case: Understanding business impact when your team/users are actively working.

üîß Work Schedule Configuration

Work Hours per Week: Total weekly work hours for impact analysis (1-168 hours).

Start/End Hours: Daily work schedule used to calculate work time periods.

Note: These settings only affect the work impact analysis, not the base SLA calculation.
